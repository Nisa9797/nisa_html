My Family

A little bit about my family:

I come from a large, loving family of eight. As the eldest daughter and third child, I share a home with four brothers and one little sisters. My father is a dedicated civil servant, while my mother lovingly tends to our home. While two of my siblings are already embarking on their professional journeys, the rest of us are still pursuing our education. Every Eid, we eagerly return to my mother's hometown to celebrate with our extended family. We also make it a tradition to choose a theme for our Raya attire and have a special family day on the second day of Eid. Here are some beautiful moments with my family. Each image represents the love and bond we share.
